Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

LoanMetric ("we", "our", or "the app") is an EMI calculator developed for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

LoanMetric does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All loan calculations and saved history are stored locally on your device using an on-device database.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data
  • App usage data

We comply with GDPR by using Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain consent before serving personalized ads to users in the EEA.

Data Storage

All calculation history and user preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data.

Permissions

The app requires only the INTERNET permission, which is used solely for loading advertisements. No other permissions are required.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – For displaying advertisements
  • Google User Messaging Platform – For GDPR consent management

Children's Privacy

LoanMetric is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
